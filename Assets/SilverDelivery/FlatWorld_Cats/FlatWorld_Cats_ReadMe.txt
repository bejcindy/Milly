Flat World - Cats - Rigged & Animated

Flat World cats rigged and animated with a simple/ flat/ unlit look to them.

CONTENTS:
4 skinned meshes
 smesh_cat_01 (black) (1606 faces, 1445 vertices, 3017 edges)
 smesh_cat_02 (white) (1606 faces, 1445 vertices, 3017 edges)
 smesh_cat_03 (grey) (1606 faces, 1445 vertices, 3017 edges)
 smesh_cat_04 (orange) (1606 faces, 1445 vertices, 3017 edges)

1 material 
 m_flatworld_cats (for all models)

1 64x64px texture 
 t_flatworld_cats (for all models)

6 animations
 cat_idle_01
 cat_idle_02
 cat_sit_01
 cat_sleep_01
 cat_sleep_02
 cat_walk_01

1 animator
 anim_flatworld_cats

HOW TO USE:
1. Go to the "prefabs" folder, drag and drop... good luck :).
2. *Animators* use paramaters to trigger animations with the same name as the paramater's name
3. *Optional* In the "materials" folder customize the colors of the materials using the color selection interface.
